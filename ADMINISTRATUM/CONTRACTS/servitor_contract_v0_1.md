## Input Contract

Servitor query must include:
- `task_id`
- `question`
- optional `mode`

## Output Contract

Return `newgen_organ_verdict_v0_1` with:
- continuity/routing applied rules;
- required closure and evidence actions;
- forbidden hygiene/sequence actions.

## BLOCK Conditions

- missing closure receipt contract;
- report/receipt paths missing for critical outputs;
- scope drift beyond declared boundaries.

## WARN Conditions

- partial continuity pack but explicitly recoverable.

- autonomous stage orchestration.
