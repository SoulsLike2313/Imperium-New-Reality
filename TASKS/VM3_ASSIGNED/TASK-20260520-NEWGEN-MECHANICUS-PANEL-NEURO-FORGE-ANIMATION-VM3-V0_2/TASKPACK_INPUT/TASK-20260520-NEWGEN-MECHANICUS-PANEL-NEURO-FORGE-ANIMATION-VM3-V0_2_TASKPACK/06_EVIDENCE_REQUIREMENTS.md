1. Officio ACK or missing-authority WARN JSON.
2. `START_ACK.md`
3. `WORK_LOG.md`
4. `GIT_STATUS_BEFORE.txt`
5. Updated implementation files.
6. `SCREENSHOTS/01_full.png`
7. `SCREENSHOTS/02_detail_*.png` (3-5 files)
8. One before/after note or comparison file.
9. `REPORTS/IMPLEMENTATION_REPORT_EN.md`
10. `REPORTS/OWNER_REPORT_RU.md`
11. `RECEIPTS/FINAL_RECEIPT_V0_2.json`
12. `GIT_STATUS_AFTER.txt`
