# Evidence Vault Batch Pack Plan Doctrine v0.1

Batch Preview is not execution. Evidence Vault Pack Plan is also not execution.

## Law

A pack plan may identify high-confidence source evidence/report candidates and compute a manifest preview, but it must not copy, move, delete, pack, archive, or rewrite source. A later execution patch requires explicit owner approval and must provide its own receipt and visual proof.

## Trinity Plus requirement

Every pack plan must include a visual proof surface showing:

- selected candidate count;
- total planned bytes;
- top affected folders;
- target Vault root preview;
- what is allowed next;
- what is explicitly blocked.

## Source deletion

Source deletion is not authorized by a pack plan. Deletion requires a separate owner gate after a sealed Vault copy exists and is verified.
