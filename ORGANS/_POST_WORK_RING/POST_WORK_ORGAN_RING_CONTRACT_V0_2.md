## V0.2 Rule

Every completed task bundle must carry all nine organ receipt rows and each row must be schema-backed. A required organ may not be omitted from final acceptance. A required organ row with `status=BLOCK` blocks the whole bundle and triggers a repair request.

- `ASTRONOMICON`
- `OFFICIO_AGENTIS`
- `ADMINISTRATUM`
- `MECHANICUS`
- `DOCTRINARIUM`
- `INQUISITION`
- `STRATEGIUM`
- `SCHOLA_IMPERIALIS`
- `CUSTODES`

## Custodes Boundary

Custodes remains a narrow organ matrix auditor. It checks ring completeness and malformed rows; it does not claim full Throne or semantic authority.
