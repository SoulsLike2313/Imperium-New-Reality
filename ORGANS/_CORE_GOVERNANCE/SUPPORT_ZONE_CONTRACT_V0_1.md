# Support Zone Contract V0.1

Status: CANDIDATE_V0_1
Owner organ: ADMINISTRATUM

## Purpose

Support zones hold shared material that serves required organs but is not itself an organ authority.

## Zones

- SUPPORT/COMMON_IMPERIUM_SUPPORT/ contains admitted common support.
- SUPPORT/QUESTIONABLE_OR_QUARANTINE/ contains material banned from active use until explicit salvage or admission.

## Classification Rule

Support material must declare owner organ, supported organs, allowed use, and evidence path. Unknown support must be recorded as UNKNOWN_WITH_REASON and routed for later classification.
