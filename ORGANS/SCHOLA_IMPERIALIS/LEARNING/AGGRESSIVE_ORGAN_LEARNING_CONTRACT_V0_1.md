# Aggressive Organ Learning Contract V0.1

Status: CANDIDATE_V0_1
Owner organ: SCHOLA_IMPERIALIS

## Purpose

Manual architectural intent must become durable organ knowledge: contracts, matrices, schemas, templates, validators, receipts, alerts, metrics, address-book seeds, and next-route notes.

## Required Capture

Every repeated manual intervention pattern must become at least one of:

- preventive rule;
- future checker hook;
- matrix row;
- template field;
- validator input.

## Boundary

Learning capture does not claim that all future failures are prevented. It records concrete hooks that can be replayed or hardened later.
