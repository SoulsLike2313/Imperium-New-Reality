# CONTINUITY_ROLE_PACK_BRIDGE_CONTRACT_V0_1

Status: `CANDIDATE_V0_1`
Owner organ: `ADMINISTRATUM`
Support organs: `OFFICIO_AGENTIS`, `INQUISITION`, `MECHANICUS`, `ASTRONOMICON`

## Principle

Continuity truth is role-neutral.  
Selected recipient role changes only behavior overlay, not project truth.

## Recipient targets

- `LOGOS_PRIME`
- `INQUISITOR`
- `SPECULUM`

## Required separation

Continuity pack (Administratum-owned) must stay stable across targets and include:

- git/repo truth probe;
- active caps snapshot;
- source index;
- stale-context audit;
- private-data exclusion receipt;
- latest accepted task chain.

Role pack (Officio-owned) may vary by target and include:

- role identity and response mode;
- first-read file list;
- review posture;
- allowed claims / stop conditions;
- cleanliness checks.

## Non-mutation rule

Role pack selection MUST NOT mutate:

- `core project truth`;
- `git/head facts`;
- `cap ledger facts`;
- `evidence boundary facts`.

## Required matrix fields

Role mapping must provide at least:

- `recipient_target`
- `role_owner_organ`
- `role_pack_path`
- `response_contract_path`
- `first_read_files`
- `review_posture`
- `cleanliness_checks`
