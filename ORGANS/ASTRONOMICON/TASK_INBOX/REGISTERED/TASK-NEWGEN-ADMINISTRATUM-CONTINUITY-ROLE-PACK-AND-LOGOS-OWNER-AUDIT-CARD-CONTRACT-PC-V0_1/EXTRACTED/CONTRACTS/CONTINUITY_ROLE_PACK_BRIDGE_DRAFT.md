# Continuity Role Pack Bridge Draft

## Contract ID

`CONTINUITY_ROLE_PACK_BRIDGE_V0_1`

## Principle

Continuity truth is role-neutral. Role behavior is selected separately.

## Recipient targets

| Target | Core continuity | Role pack owner | Review posture |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| LOGOS_PRIME | Same project truth | Officio Agentis | Continuity, planning, taskpack generation |
| INQUISITOR | Same project truth | Inquisition plus Officio | Evidence audit, fake-green detection, gate strictness |
| SPECULUM | Same project truth | Speculum profile plus Officio | Red-team architecture and technical critique |

## Required pack separation

Continuity pack must include:

- project truth;
- current heads;
- active caps;
- latest accepted tasks;
- source index;
- stale-context audit;
- private-data exclusion receipt.

Role pack must include:

- role identity;
- response contract;
- first-read order;
- allowed claims;
- stop conditions;
- review posture.

The selected role pack must not alter continuity truth.
