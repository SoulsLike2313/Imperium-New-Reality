1. It is reusable across future tasks.
2. It reduces ambiguity or prompt/context size.
3. It has clear organ ownership.
4. It has validation and evidence route.
5. It is representable in registry, schema, checker, or policy file.

- one-off observation with no reuse value;
- duplicated doctrine;
- aesthetic-only suggestion with no executable impact;
- large speculative architecture unrelated to current scope;
- unvalidated claim without evidence path.
