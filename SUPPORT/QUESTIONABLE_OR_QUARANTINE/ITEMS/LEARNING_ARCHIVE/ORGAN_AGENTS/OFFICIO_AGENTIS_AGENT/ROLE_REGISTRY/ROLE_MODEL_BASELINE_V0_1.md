# Officio Agentis Role Model Baseline V0.1

## Purpose
This baseline defines the first canonical role shape for IMPERIUM New Generation Officio Agentis.
It removes Kiro from the internal role registry and keeps only two canonical role families:

- LOGOS
- SERVITOR

Each family has two operating profiles:

- PRIME
- SPECULUM

This creates four role profiles:

- LOGOS_PRIME
- LOGOS_SPECULUM
- SERVITOR_PRIME
- SERVITOR_SPECULUM

## Core Rule
A role is not a personality and not a speech style.
A role is a behavior contract that defines mission, authority, boundaries, required inputs, read order, evidence discipline, stop conditions, outputs, and response contract.

## Prime / Speculum Pattern
Prime builds or executes the intended work.
Speculum attacks, audits, and tests the same work type.

- Logos-Prime builds the form.
- Logos-Speculum attacks the form.
- Servitor-Prime executes the task.
- Servitor-Speculum attacks the execution.

## Kiro Policy
Kiro is not an internal canonical role in Officio Agentis V0.1.
Kiro may be referenced later as an external advisory source or external tool, but not as a role profile controlled by Officio.

## Canonical Artifact Language
Canonical files, schemas, registries, receipts, logs, and machine-readable artifacts must be English by default to reduce encoding risk.
Owner-facing live chat may be Russian.
