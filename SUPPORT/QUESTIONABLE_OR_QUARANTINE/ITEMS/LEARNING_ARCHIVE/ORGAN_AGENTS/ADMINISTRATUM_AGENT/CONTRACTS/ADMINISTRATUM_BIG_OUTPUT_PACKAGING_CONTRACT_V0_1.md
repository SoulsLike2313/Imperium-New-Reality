# ADMINISTRATUM BIG OUTPUT PACKAGING CONTRACT V0.1

## Purpose
Convert large run outputs into bounded dossier packages instead of chat dumps.

## Applies to
- check-all outputs
- continuity packs
- audits
- future attack evidence packs

## Packaging requirements
- compact owner summary plus full machine receipts
- manifest and hash index
- limitations and unverified section
- traceable evidence index
- clear verdict and trust basis

## Non-goals
- no claim of universal canonization in V0.1
- no uncontrolled heavy UI/browser stack requirements
